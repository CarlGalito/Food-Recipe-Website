Beat the Heat: Halo-Halo Delight

A colorful and icy Filipino dessert that's perfect for summer days!

What is Halo-Halo?

Halo-Halo, meaning “mix-mix,” is a famous Filipino dessert made with crushed ice, sweetened fruits, jellies, beans, leche flan, and ube ice cream. It’s a sweet and refreshing treat enjoyed year-round.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Halo-Halo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 1 cup crushed ice
  • ✔ 2 tbsp sweetened banana (saba), sliced
  • ✔ 2 tbsp sweetened jackfruit (langka)
  • ✔ 2 tbsp sweetened red beans
  • ✔ 2 tbsp sweetened garbanzos
  • ✔ 2 tbsp nata de coco
  • ✔ 2 tbsp kaong (sugar palm)
  • ✔ 2 tbsp macapuno
  • ✔ 1 slice leche flan
  • ✔ 1 scoop ube ice cream
  • ✔ 2 tbsp evaporated milk

Instructions:

  1. Layer all the sweetened fruits and ingredients at the bottom of a tall glass or bowl.
  2. Top with crushed ice until full.
  3. Drizzle evaporated milk over the ice.
  4. Add a slice of leche flan and a scoop of ube ice cream on top.
  5. Serve immediately and mix before eating. Enjoy the explosion of flavors!
