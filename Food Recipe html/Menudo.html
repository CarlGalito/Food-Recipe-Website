Classic Menudo Delight

Hearty, rich, and full of flavor — your favorite Menudo recipe starts here!

What is Menudo?

Menudo is a traditional Filipino stew made with pork, liver, and vegetables simmered in tomato sauce. It’s a favorite during fiestas and everyday meals because of its comforting, rich flavor.

Menudo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 1/2 kg pork (cut into small cubes)
  • ✔ 1/4 kg pork liver (cut into small cubes)
  • ✔ 1 cup tomato sauce
  • ✔ 2 medium potatoes (cubed)
  • ✔ 1 large carrot (cubed)
  • ✔ 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • ✔ 1 green bell pepper (sliced)
  • ✔ 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • ✔ 1 onion (chopped)
  • ✔ 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • ✔ 1 tbsp calamansi or lemon juice
  • ✔ 1 cup water
  • ✔ Salt and pepper to taste
  • ✔ Cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, marinate pork with soy sauce and calamansi juice for 30 minutes.
  2. Sauté garlic and onion in oil until fragrant. Add the pork and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in tomato sauce and water. Bring to a boil then simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Add the liver, potatoes, and carrots. Cook for another 10-15 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  5. Stir in the bell peppers. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Simmer for 5 more minutes. Serve hot with rice!
