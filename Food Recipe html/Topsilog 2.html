What is Tapsilog?

It's a popular dish for breakfast but can also be enjoyed anytime during the day. The dish is often served with a side of dipping sauce like sukang may sili (vinegar with chili), which adds extra flavor.

Topsilog Variations

These variations make Topsilog a versatile and customizable Filipino breakfast dish, perfect for different tastes and preferences.

✔ Tapsilog with Tapa

This is marinated beef, typically sliced thinly, seasoned with a combination of garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and spices. It's then fried until its tender, slightly crispy on the edges, and flavorful. The sweet and savory flavor of the tapa is the highlight of the dish.

✔ Tapsilog with Sinangag

he rice is stir-fried with garlic, creating a fragrant and savory base that complements the beef tapa perfectly. Using day-old rice helps achieve the right texture and flavor.

✔ Tapsilog with Itlog

A fried egg, usually sunny-side up or scrambled, is served on top or beside the tapa and sinangag, adding richness to the dish.

Topsilog with Tapa

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 1 lb beef (sirloin or flank steak), thinly sliced
  • ✔ 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ✔ 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • ✔ 1 tbsp vinegar
  • ✔ 1 tbsp sugar
  • ✔ 1 tsp pepper
  • ✔ 1 tbsp vegetable oil (for frying)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Tapa:
    2. In a bowl, mix together garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and pepper.
  2. Add the thinly sliced beef to the marinade, making sure all pieces are coated. Marinate for at least 30 minutes (overnight for best flavor).
  3. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry the marinated beef in batches until tender and slightly crispy on the edges, about 3-5 minutes per batch. Set aside.

Tapsilog with Sinangag

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • ✔ 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ✔ 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • ✔ Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until golden brown and fragrant.
  3. Add the cold rice and stir-fry, breaking up any clumps. Continue cooking for 3-5 minutes until the rice is heated through and slightly crispy.
  4. Season with salt to taste.

Tapsilog with Itlog

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 2 eggs
  • ✔ 1 tbsp oil (for frying)

Instructions:

  1. In a separate pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium heat.
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan and fry to your preferred doneness (usually sunny-side up is common for Topsilog).
