Comfort in Every Spoon: Tinolang Manok

A soothing, gingery chicken soup perfect for any day.

What is Tinolang Manok?

Tinola is a traditional Filipino chicken soup flavored with ginger, garlic, and fish sauce. It’s commonly cooked with green papaya and chili leaves, making it nourishing and comforting.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Tinolang Manok Recipe

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 1 kg chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • ✔ 1 small green papaya (sliced)
  • ✔ 1 bunch chili leaves or malunggay
  • ✔ 1 thumb-sized ginger (sliced)
  • ✔ 5 cloves garlic (minced)
  • ✔ 1 medium onion (sliced)
  • ✔ 2 stalks tanglad (lemongrass), pounded and tied into a knot
  • ✔ 1 tbsp fish sauce (patis)
  • ✔ 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • ✔ 5 cups water
  • ✔ Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger until aromatic.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in fish sauce and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add water and tanglad (lemongrass). Bring to a boil.
  5. Lower the heat and let it simmer until the chicken is tender (about 30 minutes).
  6. Add sliced papaya and cook until softened.
  7. Stir in chili leaves or malunggay and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice!
