Tropical Delight: Mango Float

A chilled dessert layered with mangoes, cream, and graham crackers!

What is Mango Float?

Mango Float is a no-bake Filipino dessert made with sweet ripe mangoes, whipped cream, and graham crackers — perfect for cooling off on a warm day!

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mango Float Recipe

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 3 ripe mangoes (sliced thinly)
  • ✔ 1 pack graham crackers
  • ✔ 1 can (300ml) condensed milk
  • ✔ 2 packs (250ml each) all-purpose cream (chilled)
  • ✔ Crushed grahams (optional topping)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix chilled cream and condensed milk until well combined.
  2. In a container, layer graham crackers at the bottom.
  3. Spread a layer of cream mixture, then a layer of mango slices.
  4. Repeat the layering until the container is filled.
  5. Top with more mangoes or crushed grahams.
  6. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight. Serve chilled!
