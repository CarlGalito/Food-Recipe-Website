What is Bulalo?

Bulalo is a traditional Filipino beef soup made from shank and bone marrow. It is known for its rich and savory flavor, often enjoyed during cold weather.

Bulalo Variations

Bulalo can be prepared in various ways depending on regional preferences.

✔ Classic Bulalo

The traditional version with beef shank and vegetables.

✔ Bulalo with Corn

Includes corn for added sweetness and flavor.

Classic Bulalo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 1 kg beef shank (with bone marrow)
  • ✔ 10 cups water
  • ✔ 1 onion (quartered)
  • ✔ 2-3 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • ✔ 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • ✔ 2-3 potatoes (cut into quarters)
  • ✔ 1 cup corn (cut into pieces)
  • ✔ 1 cup cabbage (cut into wedges)
  • ✔ Salt to taste
  • ✔ Green onions (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, boil the beef shank in water for about 10 minutes, then discard the water to remove impurities.
  2. Add 10 cups of fresh water, onion, garlic, and peppercorns. Simmer for about 2-3 hours until the meat is tender.
  3. Add potatoes and corn, and cook until they are tender.
  4. Add cabbage and season with salt to taste. Cook for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with green onions. Enjoy your Bulalo!
