Silky Sweetness: Leche Flan

Rich and creamy custard with a golden caramel top.

What is Leche Flan?

Leche Flan is a Filipino dessert made with egg yolks, milk, and sugar, steamed to creamy perfection and topped with sweet caramel sauce.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Leche Flan Recipe

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 10 egg yolks
  • ✔ 1 can (300ml) condensed milk
  • ✔ 1 can (370ml) evaporated milk
  • ✔ 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ✔ 1 cup sugar (for caramel)

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, melt sugar over low heat until golden brown. Pour into llanera or mold.
  2. In a bowl, mix egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla. Strain the mixture for smooth texture.
  3. Pour mixture into the caramel-coated mold.
  4. Cover with foil and steam for 30–40 minutes or until firm.
  5. Let cool, then refrigerate for a few hours before serving.
