What is Sinigang na Hipon?

Sinigang na Hipon is a popular Filipino sour soup made with shrimp and a variety of vegetables. It is known for its refreshing and tangy taste, often enjoyed with steamed rice.

Sinigang Variations

Sinigang can be prepared in different ways depending on the protein used and regional preferences.

✔ Sinigang na Baboy

Uses pork as the main protein for a hearty version.

✔ Sinigang na Baka

Features beef for a rich and flavorful broth.

Sinigang na Hipon Recipe

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 500g shrimp (cleaned and deveined)
  • ✔ 8 cups water
  • ✔ 1 onion (quartered)
  • ✔ 2-3 tomatoes (quartered)
  • ✔ 1 radish (sliced)
  • ✔ 1 cup water spinach (kangkong)
  • ✔ 2-3 green chili peppers
  • ✔ 1 packet of sinigang mix (or tamarind paste)
  • ✔ Salt to taste
  • ✔ Steamed rice (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add onion and tomatoes. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  2. Add radish and cook until tender.
  3. Add shrimp and green chili peppers, and cook until shrimp turns pink.
  4. Stir in the sinigang mix (or tamarind paste) and season with salt to taste.
  5. Add water spinach and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your Sinigang na Hipon!
