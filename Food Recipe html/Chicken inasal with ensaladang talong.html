What is Chicken Inasal?

Chicken Inasal is a popular grilled chicken dish from the Visayas region of the Philippines, marinated in a mixture of vinegar, calamansi, and spices, giving it a unique flavor.

Chicken Inasal Variations

Chicken Inasal can be prepared in various ways depending on regional preferences.

✔ Classic Chicken Inasal

The traditional version marinated with vinegar and spices.

✔ Chicken Inasal with Spicy Marinade

Includes chili for an extra kick of heat.

Chicken Inasal Recipe

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 1 kg chicken (cut into pieces)
  • ✔ 1/2 cup vinegar
  • ✔ 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • ✔ 1/4 cup calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
  • ✔ 1 head garlic (minced)
  • ✔ 1 tsp black pepper
  • ✔ 1 tsp salt
  • ✔ 1/4 cup oil (for grilling)
  • ✔ Bamboo skewers (for grilling)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine vinegar, soy sauce, calamansi juice, garlic, black pepper, and salt to create the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and let it sit for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best results).
  3. Preheat the grill and brush it with oil to prevent sticking.
  4. Skewer the marinated chicken pieces and grill for about 15-20 minutes, basting with leftover marinade until cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice and Ensaladang Talong.

Ensaladang Talong Recipe

Ingredients:

  • ✔ 2 medium eggplants (grilled and peeled)
  • ✔ 1 medium onion (sliced)
  • ✔ 2 tomatoes (diced)
  • ✔ 1/4 cup vinegar
  • ✔ Salt and pepper to taste
  • ✔ Optional: chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Grill the eggplants until the skin is charred. Let them cool, then peel and slice.
  2. In a bowl, combine the sliced eggplants, onion, and tomatoes.
  3. Drizzle with vinegar and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Toss gently to combine and serve as a side dish with Chicken Inasal.
